WHY NAPHTHA?

At Naphtha Plastics, we've been supporting the plastics industry for over 40 years. With decades of experience and a commitment to customer service, we are your perfect polymer partner!

We supply a wide range of prime plastics as well as quality recycled plastic in either regrind or repro form. We also stock specialist powders for compounding and masterbatch production.

Whatever polymer you need, for whatever manufacturing process you use, we have the knowledge and experience to deliver the right material, every time.

FAQs

For most polymers we have a minimum order quantity of 25kg, though for masterbatch and additives this is reduced to 5kg.

We aim to have our standard range of polymers always in stock, so any orders received before 2pm can be delivered the next day. We can also offer timed / am deliveries if necessary.

We have many years of experience supplying polymers across a wide range of industries, and a solid understanding of the technical properties and processing behaviour of the materials we stock. While we're not moulding specialists, we're always happy to offer guidance based on what we've supplied for similar applications.

If you're developing a new product or exploring a change in material, we can often help with trial bags and support during R&D phases. We also have strong relationships with our customers and suppliers, so if we don't have the answer in-house, we may be able to connect you with someone who does.

Our aim is to be helpful, honest, and responsive. If you're unsure about the best polymer for your project, just get in touch — we'll do our best to point you in the right direction.

Just give us a call if you'd like to talk things through. You can also email us at info@naphthaplastics.co.uk or use the enquiry form on our website.

We pride ourselves on fast responses. We know that plans can change quickly: a new order can land out of the blue, or an existing one can suddenly double in size. When that happens, you need a supplier who gets back to you fast. And that's exactly what we aim to do.

We're based in Bury, North Manchester, and we deliver throughout Great Britain and Northern Ireland. We also regularly supply customers in Ireland and across Europe, and can arrange delivery to most locations through our trusted logistics partners.
