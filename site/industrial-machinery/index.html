Plastic Supplier for Industrial Machinery

Request An Order Read More About Naphtha Plastics

In the world of industrial machinery, components are expected to perform under pressure. They must resist mechanical stress, friction, heat, and chemical exposure, often over long service lives in harsh environments. At Naphtha Plastics, we supply high-performance polymers chosen to meet these exacting demands.

Our materials are used to produce a wide range of components, including:

Gears and bearing housings

Seals and gaskets

Bushes, rollers, and wear strips

Electrical and thermal insulators

Protective casings and covers

We stock and source a variety of engineering-grade polymers known for their strength, dimensional stability, and resistance to wear, including Nylons (PA6 and PA66), Acetal, Polycarbonate and ABS. Many of these materials are available as glass fibre filled, mineral filled , or impact-modified options.

We understand that consistent quality and uninterrupted supply are crucial for industrial manufacturers. Our materials are selected from trusted suppliers, and each batch is delivered with a Certificate of Authenticity (CoA) for traceability and peace of mind.

In many cases, cost-effective alternatives to virgin engineering plastics can be used without compromising quality. We offer reprocessed materials where suitable, with stable properties and reliable processability that are ideal for non-critical or internal parts.
