High-Performance Polymers for Modern Building

Plastic materials play a vital role in modern construction, offering durability, flexibility, and cost-efficiency across a wide range of applications. At Naphtha Plastics, we supply polymers that help manufacturers produce high-quality, reliable building components, efficiently and competitively.

Make An Enquiry

Typical applications among our construction-sector customers include:

Pipes and profiles

Conduits and cable management systems

Vents, spacers, and fixings

Protective and finishing trims

We work with manufacturers across the UK who produce these types of parts using both prime and recycled materials. In many cases, recycled polymers can be used without compromising performance, which helps our customers reduce material costs and improve the environmental footprint of their products.

We supply a range of durable, weather-resistant polymers suited to the demands of the built environment, including: HDPE and LDPE, PP, HIPS, ABS and PVC.

Our flexible stockholding and logistics mean we can deliver quickly and in quantities that suit your operation. From a few bags for trial runs to full truckloads for ongoing production, we aim to keep your line running with minimal delay.

With decades of experience working with construction-focused manufacturers, we understand the materials, pressures, and priorities of the sector. If you need support in selecting the right polymer for your product, or sourcing a specific grade, we're here to help.
