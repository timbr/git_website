Polymers We Supply

Request An Order Read More About Naphtha Plastics

Naphtha Plastics supplies a broad range of prime and recycled thermoplastics, serving sectors such as building products, industrial machinery, automotive, and medical products. Our stock includes dependable, process-friendly materials suited to both extrusion and injection moulding.

These are available directly from our warehouse for fast despatch. We focus on well-established brands we know perform reliably. If you need a specific grade, such as glass-filled, UV-stabilised, medical or food-approved, we can source it through our trusted European supply network.

If you’re looking for a specific polymer or don’t see what you need listed here, feel free to get in touch. We’re here to help you find the right material, quickly and reliably.

Below, you’ll find links to pages with further details about each of the polymers we supply:

ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene)

ABS is a tough, rigid thermoplastic known for its excellent impact resistance and durability.

Learn More

LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene)

LDPE is a flexible, lightweight thermoplastic known for its excellent impact resistance, chemical stability, and electrical insulation properties.

Learn More

HDPE

HDPE is a strong, durable thermoplastic with excellent impact resistance, chemical stability, and moisture barrier properties.

Learn More

Polypropylene Copolymer (PPC)

Polypropylene Copolymer (PPC) is a tough, flexible thermoplastic known for its improved impact resistance compared to homopolymer polypropylene.

Learn More

Polypropylene Homopolymer (PPH)

Polypropylene Homopolymer (PPH) is a lightweight, rigid thermoplastic with excellent chemical resistance, high tensile strength, and good processability.

Learn More

GPPS (General-Purpose Polystyrene)

General-Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS), also known as 'Crystal' polystyrene, is a rigid, transparent thermoplastic known for its clarity, ease of processing, and good dimensional stability.

Learn More

HIPS (High-Impact Polystyrene)

High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) is a tough, versatile thermoplastic that combines the ease of processing of polystyrene with enhanced impact resistance.

Learn More

Polycarbonate (PC)

Polycarbonate (PC) is a high-performance thermoplastic known for its exceptional impact resistance, optical clarity, and heat resistance.

Learn More

PC/ABS (Polycarbonate / Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene)

PC/ABS is a high-performance thermoplastic blend that combines the toughness and heat resistance of polycarbonate with the processability and impact strength of ABS.

Learn More

PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate)

Polybutylene Terephthalate (PBT) is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic known for its excellent mechanical strength, dimensional stability, and resistance to heat and chemicals.

Learn More

SAN (Styrene Acrylonitrile)

Styrene Acrylonitrile (SAN) is a transparent, rigid thermoplastic that combines the clarity and ease of processing of polystyrene with the enhanced chemical resistance and toughness of acrylonitrile.

Learn More

POM (Polyoxymethylene)

Polyoxymethylene (POM), also known as acetal, is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic known for its exceptional stiffness, low friction, and excellent wear resistance.

Learn More

Nylon 6 (PA6)

Nylon 6 (PA6) is a strong, lightweight engineering thermoplastic known for its excellent toughness, impact resistance, and wear properties.

Learn More

Nylon 66 (PA66)

Nylon 66 (PA66) is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic known for its superior strength, rigidity, and heat resistance compared to Nylon 6.

Learn More

PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate)

Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA), also known as acrylic, is a transparent, rigid thermoplastic known for its excellent optical clarity, weather resistance, and surface hardness.

Learn More

TPU / TPE

Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) and Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) are flexible, rubber-like materials known for their excellent elasticity, impact resistance, and durability.

Learn More

Masterbatch

Masterbatch is a concentrated mixture of pigments or additives encapsulated in a carrier resin, used to colour plastics or enhance their properties.

Learn More

EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate)

Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) is a flexible, rubber-like thermoplastic known for its excellent impact resistance, softness, and elasticity.

Learn More

Alongside prime materials, we offer a range of recycled options, including post-industrial regrind and reprocessed (repelletised) grades. Recycled plastics have become an essential part of the supply chain, and we’ve been working with them since the early days. Our team can advise on suitable recycled grades to meet both technical and environmental requirements.

We specialise in fast turnaround and small-volume supply. Orders placed today can often be delivered tomorrow, and we’re happy to fulfil anything from a single bag to a full pallet. All our materials are supplied with a Certificate of Authenticity (CoA), ensuring full traceability and peace of mind.

Since 1981, Naphtha Plastics has built a reputation for reliability, responsiveness and hands-on support. We offer more than just materials - we provide honest advice backed by decades of industry knowledge.
